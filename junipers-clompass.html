Juniper's Clompass

Re-enchanting learning through analog-digital hybridity

How might we re-enchant learning through analog-digital hybridity? Juniper’s Clompass turns a 1970s analog clock into a digital compass and storytelling device for children. Blending circuitry with imagination, the Clompass invites learners to navigate by stars and narrative rather than GPS alone.

Domain Curriculum & Physical Prototyping · STEAM Education · Storytelling

Designing tangible interfaces that merge scientific literacy with narrative play.

Experience Analog clock → digital compass

Hands-on exploration that encourages learners to orient themselves through story and sky.

Toolkit Arduino · Magnetometer · LCD

Modular electronics coupled with narrative prompts for classroom tinkering.

Curriculum Co-designed lesson plans

Tested with K–5 learners to strengthen spatial reasoning and creative writing.

Technical framework

  • Arduino-based microcontroller paired with a magnetometer and LCD interface.
  • Integrated STEAM curriculum on magnetism, directionality, and creative writing.
  • Co-designed lesson plans piloted with K–5 learners to strengthen spatial reasoning.

Impact

The Clompass bridges technical literacy and emotional curiosity—transforming STEM education into story-driven exploration.
