5.

Rebrew

Wetware for everyday healing

How might we disrupt the kitchen as a site of care? Rebrew merges speculative design and bio-material exploration to create rehydratable papers infused with herbs and flowers. Each sheet activates when steeped in water—transforming tea, broth, or poultice into ritual medicine.

Process

  • Conducted ethnographic interviews around domestic wellness rituals.
  • Prototyped biodegradable wetware forms balancing aesthetics and functionality.
  • Documented sensory experiences to inform an expanded material taxonomy.

Recognition

  • San Francisco Design Week 2024 — Best Student Design

Impact

Rebrew transforms the ordinary act of brewing into a multi-sensory interaction that reconnects technology, body, and nature.
